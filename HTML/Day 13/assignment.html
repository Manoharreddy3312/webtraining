The Lion And The Mouse

Read the moral of the story

one day,a lion was fast asleep in the jungle.a littile mouse was playing nearby and accidentally ran across his body,walking him up.

the lion caught the mouse with his huge paw and was about to eat him.

"please let me go,"begged the mouse."someday, i will repay your kindness."

the lion laughed."you? Help Me? that's funny!" but he let the mouse go.

some days later, the lion was caught in a hunter's net. he gnawed the ropes with his sharp teeth and set the lion free.

the mouse heard the lion's cries and quickly ran to help. He gnawed the ropes with his sharo teeth and set the lion free.

the lion was surprised and grateful to the little mouse.

Moral:

Even the smallest friend can be the biggest help.
