BIKE

Duke

KTM Duke is a family of standard / naked bikes — sporty look, upright riding posture, minimal fairing.They are manufactured by KTM (Austria) and in India by Bajaj Auto. Known for sharp handling, good performance, premium components (disc brakes, good suspension, strong build) and a style that often appeals to younger riders.
